# Tutorial 2: Building on Content from Lectures 2 and 3 (Corporate Governance and Personality)

This tutorial will be run as a very simple debate. You will work in teams to prepare arguments for and against the following propositions.

1. Limited liability is good for society.
2. Australian federal law is soft on corporate crime. 

To prepare for this tutorial, make notes about the possible arguments on your own by reflecting on the Lecture 2 and associated readings.

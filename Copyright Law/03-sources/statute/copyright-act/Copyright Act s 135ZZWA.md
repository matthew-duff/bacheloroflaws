# 135ZZWA Applying to Tribunal for review of distribution

arrangement
(1) A collecting society or a member of a collecting society may apply to the Copyright Tribunal for review of the arrangement adopted,

Division 3  Collecting societies or proposed to be adopted, by the collecting society for distributing amounts it collects in a period.
(2) If the Tribunal makes an order under section 153R varying the arrangement or substituting for it another arrangement, the arrangement reflecting the Tribunal’s order has effect as if it had been adopted in accordance with the collecting society’s rules, but does not affect a distribution started before the order was made.

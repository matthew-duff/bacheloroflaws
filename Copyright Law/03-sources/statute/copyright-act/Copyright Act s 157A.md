# 157A Tribunal must have regard to ACCC guidelines on request

(1) In making a decision on a reference or application under this
Subdivision, the Tribunal must, if requested by a party to the reference or application, have regard to relevant guidelines (if any) made by the Australian Competition and Consumer Commission.

Division 3  Applications and references to the Tribunal
(2) To avoid doubt, subsection (1) does not prevent the Tribunal from having regard to other relevant matters in making a decision on a reference or application under this Subdivision.

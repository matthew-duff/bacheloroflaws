# 43A Temporary reproductions made in the course of

communication
(1) The copyright in a work, or an adaptation of a work, is not infringed by making a temporary reproduction of the work or adaptation as part of the technical process of making or receiving a communication.
(2) Subsection (1) does not apply in relation to the making of a temporary reproduction of a work, or an adaptation of a work, as

Acts not constituting infringements of copyright in works  Division 3 part of the technical process of making a communication if the making of the communication is an infringement of copyright.

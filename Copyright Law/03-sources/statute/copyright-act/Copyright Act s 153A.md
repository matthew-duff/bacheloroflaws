# 153A Applications and referrals relating to Division 4 of Part IVA

(1) This section applies to an application or referral to the Tribunal mentioned in column 1 of an item of the table in subsection (4).
(2) The parties to the application or referral are the parties mentioned in column 2 of the item.
(3) The Tribunal must:
(a) consider the application or referral; and
(b) give the parties the opportunity to present their cases; and
(c) comply with column 3 of the item.

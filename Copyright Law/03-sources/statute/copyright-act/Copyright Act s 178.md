# 178 Crown copyright in recordings and films made under direction

of Crown
(1) Where, apart from this section, copyright would not subsist in a sound recording or cinematograph film made by, or under the direction or control of, the Commonwealth or a State, copyright subsists in the recording or film by virtue of this subsection.
(2) The Commonwealth or a State is, subject to this Part and to Part X, the owner of the copyright in a sound recording or cinematograph

Division 1  Crown copyright film made by, or under the direction or control of, the Commonwealth or the State, as the case may be.

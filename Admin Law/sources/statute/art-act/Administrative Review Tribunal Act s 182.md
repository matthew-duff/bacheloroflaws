# 182 When this Division applies

This Division applies if:
(a) an appeal is made to the Federal Court from a decision of the
Tribunal; and
(b) either:
(i) the Federal Court remits the matter to the Tribunal to be decided again; or
(ii) if the Federal Court transfers the appeal to the Federal
Circuit and Family Court of Australia (Division 2)— that court remits the matter to the Tribunal to be decided again.

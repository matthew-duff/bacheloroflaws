# 169 Applicant may publish findings

Subject to any order of the Tribunal, the applicant is entitled to publish the following, in any manner the applicant thinks fit:
(a) the Tribunal’s decision on the review;
(b) the findings of the Tribunal so far as they have been given to the applicant.

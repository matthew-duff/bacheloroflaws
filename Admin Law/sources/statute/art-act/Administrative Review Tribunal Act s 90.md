# 90 Engagement of persons to conduct dispute resolution processes

(1) The Principal Registrar may, on behalf of the Commonwealth, engage a person to conduct a kind of dispute resolution process.
(2) The Principal Registrar must not engage a person under subsection (1) unless the Principal Registrar is satisfied, having regard to the person’s qualifications and experience, that the person is a suitable person to conduct the relevant kind of dispute resolution process.

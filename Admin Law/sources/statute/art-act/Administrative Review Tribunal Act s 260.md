# 260 Resignation

(1) An appointed member may resign the appointed member’s appointment by giving the Governor-General a written resignation.
(2) The Chair of the Council may resign the Chair’s appointment as the Chair without resigning the Chair’s appointment as a Council member by giving the Governor-General a written resignation.
(3) The resignation takes effect on the day it is received by the Governor-General or, if a later day is specified in the resignation, on that later day.

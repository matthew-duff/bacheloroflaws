# 10 Members of Tribunal

(1) The Tribunal consists of the following members:
(a) the President;
(b) Deputy Presidents;
(c) senior members;
(d) general members.
(2) A Judge who is to be appointed as a member must be appointed as the President or as a Judicial Deputy President.

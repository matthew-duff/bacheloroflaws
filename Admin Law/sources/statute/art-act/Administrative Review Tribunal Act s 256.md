# 256 Acting appointments

The Minister may, by written instrument, appoint a Council member to act as the Chair of the Council:
(a) during a vacancy in the office of the Chair of the Council (whether or not an appointment has previously been made to the office); or
(b) during any period, or during all periods, when the Chair of the Council:
(i) is absent from duty or from Australia; or
(ii) is, for any reason, unable to perform the duties of the office.
Note: For rules that apply to acting appointments, see sections 33AB and 33A of the Acts Interpretation Act 1901.

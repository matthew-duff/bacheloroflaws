# 151 Order of evidence and submissions

When this section applies
(1) This section applies in relation to a proceeding for review of an intelligence and security decision.
Order of presentation
(2) Unless it determines otherwise, the Tribunal must hear initial evidence and submissions from the parties to the proceeding in the following order:
(a) first—evidence adduced and submissions made by the agency head;

Division 3  Proceedings
(b) second—evidence adduced and submissions made by relevant bodies;
(c) third—evidence adduced and submissions made by the applicant.

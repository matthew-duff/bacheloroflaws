# 147 Parties to proceedings

When this section applies
(1) This section applies:
(a) in relation to a proceeding for review of an intelligence and security decision; and
(b) instead of the following provisions:
(i) unless the decision is an exempt security record decision—section 22 (parties to proceeding for review);
(ii) section 60 (decision-makers may elect not to participate in kind of proceeding or Tribunal case event);
(iii) section 61 (decision-maker who elects not to participate may be a non-participating party to proceeding or Tribunal case event);

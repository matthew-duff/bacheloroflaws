# 22 Quorum for granting leave to appeal to the Queen in Council or

to High Court
Applications to the High Court for a certificate that a question as to the limits inter se of the Constitutional powers of the
Commonwealth and those of any State or States, or as to the limits inter se of the Constitutional powers of any two or more States, which has been decided by the High Court, is one which ought to be determined by the Queen in Council, shall be heard and determined by a Full Court consisting of not less than three Justices.
